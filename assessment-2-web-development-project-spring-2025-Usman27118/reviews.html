Diablo IV

PC PS5 XSX

Reviewed by Jessica Martinez | June 10, 2023

A Hellish Good Time

Diablo IV represents a triumphant return to form for Blizzard's iconic action-RPG series. The game masterfully blends the best elements of Diablo II's dark, gothic atmosphere with Diablo III's refined combat mechanics, creating an experience that feels both fresh and familiar...

Pros

  • Gorgeous, dark art direction
  • Deep character customization
  • Engaging endgame content
  • Excellent multiplayer integration

Cons

  • Some server issues at launch
  • Inventory management could be better
  • Story has some pacing issues
