About PUBG

The battle royale game that started a revolution

Our History

PUBG (PlayerUnknown's Battlegrounds) is a battle royale game developed and published by PUBG Corporation, a subsidiary of South Korean video game company Bluehole. The game was inspired by the 2000 Japanese film Battle Royale and released in March 2017.

Since its release, PUBG has become a cultural phenomenon, selling over 75 million copies on PC and consoles by 2023, and the mobile version has been downloaded over 1 billion times.
